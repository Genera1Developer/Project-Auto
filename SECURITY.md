## Password Management

- Enforce robust password policies (length, complexity, expiration) for account security.
- Implement multi-factor authentication for enhanced protection against unauthorized access.
- Utilize password managers to securely store and manage passwords, mitigating compromise risk.
- Conduct regular password audits to identify weak or compromised passwords, ensuring password strength and integrity.

## Access Control

- Define granular roles and permissions aligned with job responsibilities, implementing the principle of least privilege.
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to restrict access to sensitive data, minimizing unauthorized access and privilege escalation risks.
- Regularly review access privileges to ensure appropriateness and minimize privilege creep.
- Monitor user access and analyze anomalous behavior to enable early threat detection and investigation.

## Data Protection

- Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit using industry-standard algorithms, ensuring data confidentiality and integrity.
- Implement data masking techniques to obfuscate sensitive data, minimizing exposure risk and potential data breaches.
- Employ Data Loss Prevention (DLP) measures to detect and prevent unauthorized data exfiltration, safeguarding data integrity and compliance.
- Establish regular data backup and restore procedures to ensure business continuity and enable data recovery in the event of loss or breaches.

## Vulnerability Management

- Conduct vulnerability assessments using automated tools to identify potential security weaknesses and vulnerabilities.
- Prioritize and patch identified vulnerabilities promptly, reducing attack surface and mitigating risks.
- Track open vulnerabilities and monitor for emerging threats to maintain continuous vulnerability awareness.
- Conduct penetration testing to simulate real-world attacks and assess the effectiveness of security controls, identifying areas for improvement.

## Incident Response

- Establish a comprehensive incident response plan and train a designated response team to ensure effective incident handling and mitigation.
- Implement Intrusion Detection/Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS) to detect and block malicious activity, minimizing potential attack impact.
- Train staff on incident response procedures and their specific responsibilities to ensure a coordinated and efficient response.
- Conduct simulated incident response exercises to test response capabilities, identify areas for improvement, and enhance preparedness and response effectiveness.
