## Password Management

- Enforce stringent password policies (length, complexity, expiration) to enhance account security.
- Implement multi-factor authentication to bolster protection against unauthorized access.
- Utilize password managers to securely store and manage passwords, minimizing compromise risk.
- Conduct periodic password audits to identify compromised or weak passwords, ensuring password strength and integrity.

## Access Control

- Establish fine-grained roles and permissions aligned with job responsibilities, adhering to the principle of least privilege.
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to limit access to sensitive data, minimizing unauthorized access and privilege escalation.
- Periodically review access privileges to ensure appropriateness and mitigate privilege creep.
- Monitor user access and analyze anomalous behavior for timely threat detection and investigation.

## Data Protection

- Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit using industry-standard algorithms to ensure data confidentiality and integrity.
- Implement data masking techniques to obscure sensitive data, minimizing exposure risk and potential data breaches.
- Employ Data Loss Prevention (DLP) measures to detect and prevent unauthorized data exfiltration, protecting data integrity and compliance.
- Establish robust data backup and restore procedures to ensure business continuity and facilitate data recovery in the event of loss or breaches.

## Vulnerability Management

- Conduct regular vulnerability assessments using automated tools to identify potential vulnerabilities and security weaknesses.
- Prioritize and patch vulnerabilities promptly to reduce the attack surface and mitigate risks.
- Maintain an up-to-date vulnerability inventory and monitor for emerging threats to ensure continuous vulnerability awareness.
- Conduct penetration testing to simulate real-world attacks and evaluate the effectiveness of security controls, highlighting areas for improvement.

## Incident Response

- Establish a comprehensive incident response plan and train a dedicated team to ensure efficient incident handling and mitigation.
- Implement Intrusion Detection/Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS) to detect and block malicious activity, minimizing potential attack impact.
- Train staff on incident response procedures and their respective responsibilities to facilitate a coordinated and effective response.
- Conduct simulated incident response exercises to test response capabilities, identify improvement areas, and enhance preparedness and response effectiveness.
