## Our Pledge

We as developers, contributors, and users of this web proxy project pledge to make participation in this community a welcoming, respectful, and ethical experience for all, regardless of their skill level, experience, or personal background.

## Our Standards

* **Be Respectful:** Treat others with kindness and consideration, even if you disagree with them.
* **Avoid Harassment:** Do not engage in any form of harassment, including verbal, physical, or online abuse.
* **Foster Inclusivity:** Create an atmosphere where all participants feel welcome and valued, regardless of their gender, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, disability, or any other defining characteristic.
* **Promote Responsible Use:** Use this proxy project in a responsible manner, respecting the privacy and security of others.
* **Communicate Constructively:** Express yourself respectfully and focus on constructive criticism rather than personal attacks.
* **Report Inappropriate Behavior:** If you witness any violation of our Code of Conduct, report it to the project maintainers or community leaders immediately.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior that will not be tolerated in our community include:

* Hate speech, discriminatory language, or racial slurs
* Sexual harassment or assault
* Cheating or violating project ethics
* Bullying, trolling, or intentionally upsetting others
* Personal attacks or threats of violence

## Enforcement

Project maintainers and community leaders will review reports of unacceptable behavior and take appropriate action, including:

* Issuing warnings
* Suspending or banning users
* Removing offensive content
* Working with law enforcement if necessary

## Our Commitment to Openness and Transparency

We believe that an open and transparent development process is essential for a successful and ethical web proxy project. We encourage community members to participate in the project's development and decision-making, and we welcome feedback and contributions from all.

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness any violation of our Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers at [email protected] All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially.

### Additional Guidelines

In addition to our Code of Conduct, we also have the following guidelines in place:

* **Privacy Policy:** We respect your privacy and will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
* **Terms of Service:** By using this web proxy project, you agree to be bound by our Terms of Service.
* **Refund Policy:** We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis. For more information, please contact the project maintainers at [email protected]

## Project Specific Guidelines

In addition to our general Code of Conduct, the following specific guidelines apply to this web proxy project:

* **Responsible Proxy Use:** Use the proxy responsibly, respecting the bandwidth and resources of the host servers.
* **No Malicious Activities:** Do not use the proxy for illegal or malicious activities.
* **Respect Copyright Laws:** Do not access or distribute copyrighted material without permission.
* **Be Original:** Do not copy or plagiarize the work of others.

We reserve the right to modify or update these guidelines at any time.
